Who Am I?

Heya! My name is Jess, I'm a 23 year old Software Engineering Graduate from Nottingham Trent University.

I try and spend as much of my time as possible learning new things about computers, whether that be through working with computer hardware, or through writing software.

Computers are my passion, and the projects I take on, to me, are what that passion inspires. From my work all throughout university, to the projects I have taken on afterwards.

Some of my recent projects include trying to recreate one of my beloved childhood games in C++/SDL, along with the behemoth task of creating my own operating system from scratch!
